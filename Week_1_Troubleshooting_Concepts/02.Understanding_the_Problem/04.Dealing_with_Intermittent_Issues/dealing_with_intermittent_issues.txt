Depending on what the problem is, you might want to look at different sources of information,
- the load on the computer, 
- the processes running at the same time, 
- the usage of the network
and so on...

This is an especially annoying type of intermittent issue, nicknamed Heisenbug, in honor of Werner Heisenberg. He's the scientist that first described the observer effect, where just observing a phenomenon alters the phenomenon.
